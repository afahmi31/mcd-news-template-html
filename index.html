Macau Open 2024: Alwi Farhan overcomes Malaysian representative

kamis 26, agustus 2024 - 12.34 WIB

Bagikan :

Indonesian men's singles, Alwi Farhan, crushed Malaysian representative Cheam June Wei in the last 32 of the Macau Open 2024. He won two straight games 21-17 and 22-20 in 45 minutes. The positive result brought Alwi Farhan to the round of 16. Next, he will meet another Malaysian representative, Justin Hoh. Competing at the Macao East Asian Games Dome, Wednesday (9/25/2024) afternoon WIB, Alwi performed poorly at the start of the match. However, slowly but surely the 19-year-old player can keep up with Cheam's game and get closer at 7-8 and 9-10. In fact, he finally equalized to 11-11. After that, Cheam got additional points thanks to the variety of cross ball attacks he launched so that he returned to the lead 14-12. However,

Alwi was able to get four consecutive points to turn 18-15 ahead thanks to a series of hard and deadly smashes. Not only that, Alwi continued to make Cheam not move until he reached a score of 20-15. The player from Solo finally succeeded in closing the first game with a 21-17 victory. At the start of the second game, Alwi immediately stepped on the gas attacking with accurate sharp balls. As a result, he excelled 6-2 first. However, The Pelatnas PBSI player can catch up again in the position 13-13. The action of buying and selling attacks and chasing numbers at a fast tempo continued to occur until it reached the score 20-20. In the end, Alwi was able to get two points which made him lock in victory with a score of 22-20 in the second game.
